My real name is Glenn Lanzer III, but I go by the name of Trey. I was born in Oakland, California, but my family and I moved to the fun town of Denton, Texas when I was a one year old; and that's where I've stayed since. Denton is a thriving town of 120,000 people and is only about 30 miles north of both Dallas and Ft. Worth. I'm a student at the University of North Texas studying Mechanical and Energy Engineering as well as a student at Free Code Camp. Programming is something I very much enjoy and want to continue. Languages I'm familiar with include: HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, and jQuery. I strive to learn these core languages the best I can as well as learn other languages such as, Python, Java, and Ruby. I love to learn. Feel free to follow me on social media.
